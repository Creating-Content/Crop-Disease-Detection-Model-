About This Application

This application is designed to assist farmers and gardeners in identifying plant diseases and recommending suitable crops based on environmental conditions. By leveraging advanced AI models for image analysis and environmental data processing, it provides insights into plant health and offers practical solutions.

Our goal is to empower users with quick and accurate information, helping them to maintain healthier crops and optimize their agricultural practices. Upload an image of your plant to get an instant diagnosis, or input your local environmental data to discover the best crops for your region.
